## Trading Logics

### Long Position

- Positive momemtum
- 50SMA above 200SMA
- Entry: 55 days high breakout
- Stop: 2 * ATR or breakout of 20 days low

### Short Position

- Negative momentum
- 50SMA below 200SMA
- Entry: 55 days low breakout
- Stop: 2 * ATR or breakout of 20 days high
